Atlanta is the capital city of Georgia. It was founded in 1837 at the end of a Western & Atlantic railroad line. First named Marthasville after the then-governor’s daughter, and later changed to ‘Atlanta’, the feminine of Atlantic. Atlanta is famous nationally for its strong ties to the civil rights movement and known internationally for hosting the 1996 Olympics.
