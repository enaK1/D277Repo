Savannah is known as “The Hostess City of the South”, is the oldest city in Georgia. It was established in 1733 by General James Oglethorpe. It was a valuable asset in the American Revolution as strategic port city. Today, Savannah is known for its Historic District by tourists, and as a thriving industrial metro by the citizens.
